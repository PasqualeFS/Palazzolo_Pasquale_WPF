My Industry - Email Marketing & Design

I currently work in email marketing for the travel industry. Each day I design emails and code them with HTML and inline styles. Due to the smaller size of email preview panes, we design for a maximum width of 600 pixels. There's a good deal of calculation involved in all of this. You find yourself checking and re-checking your container widths making sure the total doesn't exceed 600 from left to right.

This is a basic calculator designed to calculate leftover space to be utilized.
